Menstrual Hygiene

Education, Sanitary Products, and Breaking Taboos

Introduction

Menstrual hygiene refers to safe and hygienic management of menstruation. It is essential for women's health, comfort, and dignity. Yet, in many communities, periods are still surrounded by stigma, secrecy, and poor access to menstrual products. Empowering women through menstrual hygiene education is a key step toward gender equality and public health.

Why Menstrual Hygiene is Important

  • Prevents reproductive and urinary tract infections
  • Improves comfort, confidence, and mobility during periods
  • Reduces school and work absenteeism
  • Promotes reproductive health and well-being
